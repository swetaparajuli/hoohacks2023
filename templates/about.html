About Us



The purpose of Break The Silence is to empower sexual assault and harassment survivors.


We are a platform where sexual assault and sexual harassment survivors can share their story, receive support, and connect with other survivors. Resources on mental health support and potential next steps would also be provided. Our mission is to empower survivors to speak about their experiences while also raising awareness on the impact of sexual violence and foster a community of support.
